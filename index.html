AGENTS OF TIME
PRESENT

OBSCURA NEW IMPRINT

Andrea Di Ceglie, Fedele Ladisa and Luigi Tutolo aka Agents Of Time recently joined their forces to launch Obscura, a new venture born from their eponym party series dedicated to release some of those analog compositions they are known for, alongside works from producer they matter.

First in the line is the Bari based trio, serving up four new original tracks through the already out Day One EP. Balancing between hypnotic sequences, dark and obscure landscapes, paced by powerful drums and magnetic synths, it showcases what the three italians are capable of during their unique live set, as their taste for strongly layed out Techno.

Available on wax and digital, Obscura001 draw the first lines of a new story Agents Of Time have to tell us. Exciting !
