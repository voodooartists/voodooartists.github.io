DISTANT IMAGES EP
OUT NOW ON ANTINOTE !

Over the years and releases, D.K.’s take on ambient music never stopped to get more precise, more personal, more obsessive. This fourth release for the Parisian imprint steps perfectly into that logic.

With melodies gliding between the clouds and the stars, the sky and heaven, "Distant Images" draws a fuzzy line between dreams and reality as piano, guitar, synths and gamelan rhythms respond to each other through the EP.

It might be D.K. most accomplished work to date, a guenine expression of his inner quest and influences, including Steve Reich, Gigi Masin and his recent collaborator Suzanne Kraft.

D.K. JOINS SUZANNE KRAFT AS D.K./S.K. !

D.K. REMIX THE RANGE ON DOMINO RECORDS

Sharing the same penchant for the spacier side of electronic music, D.K. and Suzanne Kraft naturally combines on D.K./S.K., EP released through Jonny Nash's label Melody As Truth.

Alongside reinterpretations from Kenton Slash Demon and South Korea / NY's phenomenon Yaeji, The Range's "Remixes EP" features four new looks on their last EP and first album.
