Here we are, to speak about Innervision, the new EP from Parisian House and Jazz champion Neue Grafik freshly released on Peckham's imprint, responsible for some of the best groovy materials to listen out there, Rhythm Section International !

Made of six crispy Broken Beat gems, with a spectacular eponymous intro featuring the soul nourished voice of his long time friend Wayne Snow, this debut piece on Bradley Zero's label plays on the same scale with Hip-Hop and club behaviors amongst Neue's many influences. A perfect fit to the label catalog.

Following releases on Beat X Changers, Sampling As An Art, Wolf Music and 22a, he kept the joyful nature of his work around with a unique narrative that calls Barbès, South London and some lively dancefloor rides !
